# Digital Assets Clause for Last Will and Testament

## Clause for Inclusion in Will:

"I hereby grant my Executor full authority to access, manage, and distribute my digital assets, including but not limited to: cryptocurrencies, non-fungible tokens (NFTs), digital wallets, and online accounts. My Executor shall follow the instructions contained in my Digital Asset Inventory document dated [DATE]."

## Important Notes:
- DO NOT include private keys or passwords in this will
- Reference a separate secure document for technical instructions
- Ensure your Executor is technically capable or has access to technical advisors
